1. SERVICE OVERVIEW

The App is part of a service designed to (i) enable registered users who are in Govt quarantine or home quarantine to check their temperature periodically. (ii) to function as an indication of whether or not a user has been infected or is likely to have been infected. (iii) provide users useful information in relation to COVID-19, and (iv) to allow Govt agencies to monitor and track registered user that they are following quarantine guidelines. When the App is installed on your mobile or handheld device, it connects with the CWatch smart wearable when your device comes within Bluetooth range of CWatch device and initiates a protocol by which the information specified in the Privacy Policy (including location information) about the registered user is collected. In the event your temperature raises to a threshold, the Govt agency will contact and/or inform such registered users, to administer the appropriate medical intervention.
